└──────────────┬──────────────────────────┘
               │
┌──────────────▼──────────────────────────┐

### Tech Stack:
- **AI Models**: GPT-4 (content), Claude (analysis), DALL-E (images)
- **ML**: TensorFlow/PyTorch for prediction models
- **Data**: PostgreSQL + Redis for caching
- **Queue**: Celery for background jobs
- **Scheduling**: APScheduler for auto-posting
